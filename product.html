About Us

We at Hanumant impex believe in long-term value creation and growth not just to our customers but also to all stakeholders in the supply chain including our suppliers and employees. For this trust and good business practices are embedded in our DNA. We are among the few companies which procure directly from producers helping them grow with us. With the team having experience of over a decade in supply chain management we constantly strive to export quality and value added products to our customers across the globe.

Hanumant Impex under the brand name of “KESAR” deals in agri commodities, eco-friendly items and FMCG products with constant focus on assured premium quality merchandise at the competitive price with assured prompt delivery. Our mission is to be the best value creator for the food processing business in India. We truly believe in thinking local and acting global and are proud to sell products grown in India to the rest of the world.
